About Margarita Celeste

Margarita Celeste is an embodied astrologer, Jungian therapist, and conscious relationships guide.
She sacredly blends ancient astrological wisdom with modern somatic and psychological practices. With training in Traditional Astrology, Jungian depth psychology, Tantra, and yoga, Margarita offers a powerful, feminine, temple-like experience.

  • All readings are conducted online via Zoom. Face-to-face readings can be arranged in Cape Town between October and May.
  • Written reports and/or charts of your reading may be requested for an additional fee.
  • Accurate birth details (date, time, and location) are required for all readings. Please inform me if you do not have this information.

Refined Reading

A detailed, structured analysis based on your birth chart. We explore specific questions and themes with a focus on practical application and understanding. This is a more prepared, in-depth session.

Intuitive Reading

A spontaneous, flowing session guided by the energies present in the moment. We rely on intuition and the immediate connection to your chart, allowing insights to emerge organically without extensive prior preparation.
